A Pre-School Where Children Are Encouraged to Be Curious

At Columbus Pre-School, we believe that all young learners should be respected, valued, and encouraged to investigate the world around them. We provide a private preschool education rich in experiential learning, intentional exploration, and meaningful social-emotional development.

Our Pre-School

See What We’re All About at Columbus

At Columbus Pre-school, children feel nurtured and safe in their school environment.

With the help of teachers and the environment as the “third teacher,” students have opportunities to confidently take risks in all areas of their development.

No matter their individual learning style and unique contributions, students know that they are integral, important members of their class and school community.

Hello and Welcome to Columbus Pre-School!

I am excited to introduce you to the many wonderful things about Columbus Pre-School that made me want to join as director 12 years ago.

Our sun-drenched classrooms, gymnastics studio, and large outdoor space provide diverse experiences that support every aspect of child development. Our nurturing, dedicated early childhood educators deeply respect children, and it shows, from the care put into the aesthetics and functionality of our classrooms, to the provocations set up to spark creativity, from the way students’ ideas fuel our emergent curricula, to the thoughtful documentation and displays of student work.

As the parent of a graduate, I know first-hand how the foundation laid here at Columbus helped make my daughter, Marlow, into a kind and inclusive friend and a creative and confident student.

I hope you to visit to see for yourself the joy of our students as they play, dance, move, learn and grow here at Columbus Pre-School.

Julie Kandall, julie@columbuspre-school.com
